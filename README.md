# Craving Crusher ✊

A gamified quit-smoking coach app. Crush cravings, earn XP, level up, unlock badges, and track your health recovery.

## Features

- **🎮 XP & Leveling** — Earn XP for every craving you crush. Level up as you progress.
- **🏆 Badges** — Unlock 12 achievements from First Step to Centurion.
- **💚 Health Timeline** — Watch your body heal in real time (20 min → 1 year milestones).
- **⚡ Daily Challenges** — 4 challenges every day for bonus XP.
- **💬 AI Coach** — Real-time craving support powered by Claude.
- **🫁 Breathing Exercise** — 4-4-6 technique to kill cravings on the spot.
- **📝 Journal** — Track moods and triggers to understand your patterns.

## Deploy (2 minutes)

1. Go to [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
2. Drag this folder onto the page
3. Get your live URL
4. Add to your phone's home screen

## AI Coach Setup (optional)

Get an API key at [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com). The app works without it using offline motivational messages.
